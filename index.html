What is MicroIntern?

MicroIntern bridges the gap between learning and professional experience by offering short-term, project-based internships that help junior developers build real-world skills and portfolios.

Real Projects

Work on actual business problems with real South African companies, not just practice projects.

Flexible Timeline

Projects range from a few days to a few weeks, fitting around your schedule.

Get Paid

Earn money while gaining experience and building your professional network.
